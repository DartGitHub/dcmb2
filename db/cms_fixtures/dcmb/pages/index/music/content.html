Music =====

The DCMB plays a wide variety of music, ranging from the occasional march to popular songs to movie and video game music and more. Some of our favorites include "Hey Baby", "Build me up Buttercup", "The Muppets Theme", "Pirates of the Caribbean", and "Time Warp". In addition to these, in our weekly parade, while marching on and off the field, and whenever the football team scores, we play the school fight songs, aka "Dartmouth Tunes" (DT's). * Dartmouth's in Town Again * Come Stand Up Men * As the Backs Go Tearing By * Glory to Dartmouth Here are a few more Dartmouth songs the band either plays or sings: * Dear Old Dartmouth (Alma Mater) * Son of a Gun for Beer * Pea Green Freshmen
